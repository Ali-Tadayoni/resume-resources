# Third-Party Resume Tools

A curated list of useful tools and resources for creating, improving, and reviewing your resume.

---

# Resume Builders

### Google Docs

Free resume templates that are clean, ATS-friendly, and easy to customize.

**Website:** https://docs.google.com/

> This is the template I personally used to create my resume.

---

### ResumeMaker

A free online resume builder with several pre-designed templates.

**Website:** https://www.resumemaker.online/

---

### FlowCV

A modern resume builder with clean templates and an intuitive editor.

**Website:** https://flowcv.com/

---

# Resume Scoring & ATS Checkers

### Jobscan

Compares your resume against a job description and provides an ATS compatibility score along with improvement suggestions.

**Website:** https://www.jobscan.co/

---

### Resume Worded

Provides detailed feedback on your resume and LinkedIn profile. Some advanced features require a paid plan.

**Website:** https://resumeworded.com/

---

# Resume Inspiration

### Cake

Browse a collection of successful engineering resumes for inspiration on layout, wording, and structure.

**Website:** https://www.cake.me/featured-resumes?q=Engineering

---

### This Resume Does Not Exist

An AI-generated collection of resume examples that can be useful for inspiration.

**Website:** https://thisresumedoesnotexist.com/

> I haven't personally used this tool yet, but it looks like an interesting source of ideas.
