# Resume Checklist

- [ ] Use a pre-designed resume template.
- [ ] Make the resume fit on one page.
- [ ] Include keywords from the job description.
- [ ] Include the company name you are applying to.
- [ ] Does the first item on your resume reflect what the employer is looking for?
- [ ] Use experience titles that demonstrate value.
- [ ] Include links to your online profiles (e.g., LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio).
- [ ] Remove the word "I."
- [ ] Avoid buzzwords that simply describe how great you are.
- [ ] Use strong action verbs.
- [ ] Measure your impact with quantifiable results instead of only describing responsibilities.
- [ ] Ensure your technical skills section includes the technologies the employer is looking for. Only include years of experience if they strengthen your application.
- [ ] Include only your strongest sections and items (e.g., Experience, Projects, Education, Technical Skills).
- [ ] Check for typos and grammar mistakes.
